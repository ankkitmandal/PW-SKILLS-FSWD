Q4. Write a Short note on Tag and element with an example.

1.A HTML Tag is composed of annotation or symbols, that provides structure to the text document.
2.A HTML Element is composed of starting tag, content, ending tag ,it compiles tag and content to provide
  structure to the text document. 

Example- <h>Hello Moto</h> ,here <h> & </h> are starting and ending tag respectively, and Hello Moto is the 
content between those tags.
