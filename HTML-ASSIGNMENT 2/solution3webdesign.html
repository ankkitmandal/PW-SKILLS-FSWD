Web Design

Web design focuses on creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. It combines elements of graphic design, layout, and user experience to craft engaging digital interfaces. Web designers use tools like Adobe XD, Sketch, or Figma to create wireframes and prototypes, and they work closely with web developers to turn designs into functional websites. Key considerations include color schemes, typography, layout, and responsive design to ensure a seamless user experience across devices. Effective web design enhances a website's aesthetics, usability, and overall effectiveness in conveying information or facilitating interactions.
